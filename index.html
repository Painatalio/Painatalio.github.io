Our Goal

The objective of Happy Population is to provide insights of how a population, in a country, perceives themselves in terms of happiness, and what factors influence their happiness.

What is the World Happiness Report

The World Happiness Report is a landmark survey of the state of global happiness.

What is the cartil laddder

The cartil ladder is a measure of life satisfaction where O is the distopia country, where there is the worst possible, and 10 is the utopia country, where everything is perfect.
